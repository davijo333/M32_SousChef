# Sous Chef — Naming Conventions

Final naming locked for the M32 take-home project.

## Official Names

| Use | Name |
|-----|------|
| **Product name** | Sous Chef |
| **Project / repo folder** | `M32_SousChef` |
| **Package / app slug** | `sous-chef` |
| **MongoDB database** | `sous_chef` |
| **Browser tab title** | Sous Chef |

## Tagline

**Your AI sous chef for menu & inventory.**

Alternates:
- *From supplier bill to reorder list.*
- *Everything in its place.*

## One-Liner

Sous Chef helps small café owners turn supplier bills and sales receipts into live inventory, menu insights, and reorder advice — through upload, review, and chat.

## Usage Rules

- **User-facing copy:** always "Sous Chef" (two words, title case).
- **Code / URLs / repos:** `sous-chef`, `sous_chef`, or `M32_SousChef` — never `SousChef` mashed together in UI.
- **Short reference in chat:** "Sous Chef" or "the app" — not "Sous" alone (avoids confusion with the French word in isolation).
